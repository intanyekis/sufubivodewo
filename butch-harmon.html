    Butch Harmon- Biography

    Butch Harmon was born in New York on August 28, 1943. Renowned golf teacher and former golfer most famous for having coached Tiger Woods from 1993 to 2004. He is the author of books like “Four Cornerstones of Winning Golf” and has written a regular column in Golf Digest magazine. Butch coached Tiger Woods during the golfer’s meteoric rise through the sport. He spent three years in the U.S. Army and served in the Vietnam War.
