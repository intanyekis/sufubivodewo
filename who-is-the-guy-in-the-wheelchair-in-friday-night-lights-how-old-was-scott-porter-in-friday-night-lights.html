    Who Is Scott Porter?

    Scott Porter is a screen and voice actor famous for portraying Jason Street in the TV series “Friday Night Lights” (2006-2010).

    Born on July 14, 1979, in Omaha, Nebraska, United States, Porter has lent his voice to several video games and animated series.

    He voiced the character of Luke Skywalker in the animated series “Star Wars: The Clone Wars” and Cyclops in the animated series “X-Men: Evolution.” He also voiced video games like “Batman: Arkham Origins” and “Marvel Heroes.”

    Porter has so far appeared in several feature films and TV series such as “Music and Lyrics” (2007), “Prom Night” (2008), “Dear John” (2010), “The To Do List” (2013), “The Spectacular Now” (2013), “The Good Wife” (2010-2016), “Hart of Dixie” (2011-2015), “Law & Order: SVU,” “Scorpion,” “Lucifer,” and “Grimm.”

    In 2023, Porter played a recurring role as Orson in the TV series Up Here.
