    Chris Brown is a critically-acclaimed American singer, songwriter, dancer, and actor with a gargantuan $50 million net worth. The musical sensation is known globally as one of the most influential R&B singers on the planet, with his musical style often described as 'Polyhedric' by several established critics and entertainment executives. Brown is a proud recipient of a triple platinum studio album and is often compared with the likes of other legendary musical artists like Michael Jackson due to his style and immense popularity.

    Chris Brown's successful stint in the music industry has translated to the artist living the high-life. Chris owns some of the world's most extravagant automobiles and luxury mansions that are worth a fortune. However, Brown's obsession with sneakers stands out from the rest of his interests, with his enormous collection of some of the world's most expensive and revered kicks displaying his unique and highly distinctive way of life.

    Supreme X Nike Air More Uptempo

    Nike is known globally for its brilliantly designed and highly customized kicks. The American shoemaker has partnered with an ensemble of world-renowned celebrities and professional athletes to treat sneaker-lovers worldwide with a vast and varied range of limited edition kicks that scream desirability.

    Chris Brown fits the bill as one of the world's most passionate sneaker-heads on the planet, and his mind-boggling collection is home to the stunning 'Supreme X Nike Air More Uptempo'.

    This gorgeous pair of retro runners feature a tonal metallic gold upper finished in rich leather, as well as the word 'Supreme' embossed in high relief leather patches that wrap around the sides and heel counter.

    The style quotient surrounding these marvelous kicks gets stronger still as Nike's coveted 'Swoosh' logo, embroidered in red, makes its presence felt on the outsole.

    Additionally, the generously thick midsole with classic Air Max units gives the sneakers an instantly recognizable look as well as top-notch levels of comfort, making these exquisite kicks all-rounders in the segment.

    With the swanky kicks making an appearance at the sneaker retail giant 'Flight Club' for an astounding $3,000, the desirability quotient surrounding these sneakers remains at an all-time high. Moreover, its selling price of a whopping $190 is justified owing to the story behind its conception and immense desirability factor.

    Adidas Yeezy' BOOST' 350 V2

    Adidas takes the style dynamic of its sneaker range to a whole new level. The German multinational corporation is renowned for its contribution to the sports industry on a global scale, with the firm's apparel, footwear, and sportswear holding high regard in the minds of a variety of celebrities and professional athletes.

    The famous 'Yeezy' range of sneakers is undeniably the brand's most successful offering. The 'Yeezy' nameplate holds a tremendous market value in the eyes of many, and Adidas has flawlessly capitalized on this popularity by successfully innovating their product portfolio to keep up with the barrage of evolving trends and requirements in the market.

    The German firm's 'Yeezy BOOST 350 V2' is yet another addition to the 'Yeezy' assortment of sneakers and has once again managed to capture the interest of sneakerheads the world over with its eye-catchy design, exorbitance, and enormous comfort factor.

    The stylish pair of kicks sport an upper derived from re-engineered Primeknit as well as a monofilament side stripe that is woven into the upper itself. The wow factor surrounding the sneakers gets stronger still, with reflective threads woven into its laces, giving it a one-of-a-kind appearance.

    With the midsole of the kicks featuring the brand's coveted 'BOOST' technology, Adidas has managed to keep their 'Yeezy' range of sneakers as desirable as ever. At an asking price of $220, Chris Brown's collection only seems to get more enthralling.

    Air Jordan Black Spiz'ike: 'Black History Month'

    The story behind the conception of these kicks makes them monumentally unique. The 'BHM 2013' edition of these sneakers was launched in February 2013 as part of the Nike range to honor 'Black History Month'.

    These exquisitely designed sneakers sport various elements from the Air Jordan 3,4,5 and 6 range, with its 'hybrid' design philosophy working wonders for its overall appearance and comfort characteristics.

    The 'Spiz'ike' kicks get their name from renowned American film producer and director Spike Lee who played a significant role in the early success and commercial appeal of the Air Jordan range of sneakers.

    The 'Volt Monochromatic' color scheme gives the sneakers an intriguing appearance, while the Air Jordan 3 inspired elephant pattern present on the heel and toe of the shoe adds to its aesthetic appeal that much more.

    Additionally, the 'Spiz'ike' kicks also feature plastic 'Wings' lace holders derived from the Air Jordan 4 as well as netting on the mid-panel taken straight from the Air Jordan 5. The icing on the nostalgia cake is the Air Jordan 6 inspired neoprene lace cover with a photo blue colored 'BHM' embroidery that gives these particular pair of kicks a well-deserved appreciation factor.

    With a price tag north of $180, these groovy kicks are yet another sweet addition to Chris Brown's stupendous collection.

    Public School Air Jordan XXII

    The legacy of the Air Jordan range of sneakers seems unrelenting, with the addition of the Public School Air Jordan XXII polishing further the aura of desirability surrounding the critically acclaimed collection of kicks.

    This particular version of the Air Jordan series was conceived via the collaboration between Maxwell Osborne and Dao-Yi Chow's Public school fashion label and the Jordan brand.

    The very first 'Dark Gray' colorway edition of the kicks would make their debut in 2015, with the additional Wheat, Bordeaux, and Olive editions accentuating further the immense desirability factor surrounding the fashionable sneakers.

    With a price tag north of $300, these uber-stylish pair of kicks add a dollop of excitement to Chris Brown's humongous collection.

    Nike Air Yeezy 2

    These swanky kicks encompass an immense market value and an unparalleled level of desirability amongst sneakerheads globally.

    Their stylish design resembles the likes of its predecessor, the 'Air Yeezy Blink', with the more significant changes coming in the form of a refined silhouette and several unique details crafted especially for American musical artist, Kanye West.

    The meticulous attention to detail surrounding the kicks includes a molded rubber heel; an anaconda textured side panel and even particular Egyptology references on the tongue and midfoot of the strap.

    With a price tag north of $4,000, this special set of Yeezys is undeniably one of the more extravagant pieces in Chris Brown's decorated collection.

    Deadstock Air Jordan 11: 'Space Jam'

    This edition of the coveted Air Jordan series of sneakers is widely regarded as one of the rarest and most desirable pairs of kicks ever made.

    With only a handful of these beauties in existence, this Air Jordan range of kicks edition is perhaps the most exorbitant in Chris Brown's collection.

    The sneakers were developed after the monumental success of the 1996 American-live action comedy movie 'Space Jam' which saw the appearance of Michael Jordan and several other big-money basketball players.

    The design of the sneakers was undertaken by legendary Nike design professional Tinker Hatfield, who designed the shoe to optimize comfort and ease of movement during gameplay for Michael Jordan.

    With an esteemed market value north of a whopping $160,000, Chris Brown certainly has a keen eye for quality, rarity, and extravagance.

    Chris Brown is undeniably one of the most accomplished musical artists in the world. The American singer drips panache and certainly knows how to live the high-life with his $50 million fortune. His mind-boggling sneaker collection is undeniably one of his most unique hobbies, with his passionate outlook on the segment pushing him to add a variety of other limited edition kicks that possess rarity, extravagance, and an unparalleled sense of desirability. However, Chris Brown's individuality helps him move away from the list of traditional sneaker collectors, as the musical genius makes use of the enormous variety of kicks in his collection for concerts and everyday use. The hit American singer's popularity is very much on the rise, and one can expect his colorful collection of sneakers to get a tad more exciting shortly.
