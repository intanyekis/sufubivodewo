    William H. Macy and Felicity Huffman are a Hollywood match made in heaven. The couple met in the early 80s and dated on and off for 15 years before making things official in 1997. Daughter Sophia came into the world in 2000 and Georgia followed in 2002.

    Though the picture-perfect family, William, Felicity and the girls endured controversy in 2019 when the “Varsity Blues” college admissions scandal emerged. Felicity was caught paying $15,000 to doctor her daughter Sophia’s SAT scores. She eventually pleaded guilty to fraud and served 11 days behind bars.
