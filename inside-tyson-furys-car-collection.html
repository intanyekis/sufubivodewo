    Tyson Fury is a 31-year-old English professional boxer who happens to be a two-time world heavyweight champion with a WBC title and The Ring magazine title which he held for two years. He is the third heavyweight champion after Mohammed Ali and Floyd Patterson to hold The Ring magazine title for 2 years and he has a net worth of $65 million. He is a successful professional boxer by all standards as he has been ranked as the best active heavyweight in the world by ESPN and so many other notable rankings. While his success and reputation precede him, the Gypsy King as he calls himself has a breathtaking car collection that would be worth over $2 million and that is definitely a thing to be talked about. Let’s have a look at his luxurious car collection.

    1993 Classic Mini Cooper ($13,500)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    This car ranks as the silliest and most ridiculous car in Tyson’s luxurious car collection largely due to its tiny cute size in comparison to Tyson’s 6ft height and hefty weight but despite that, it still remains a classic, and amazing to date even though it has now gone into extinction from any modern showroom. Tyson’s 1993 Mini Cooper is painted in red and has a white roof and seeing Tyson in it sure caused a funny frenzy with lots of laughs on social media and even for his then 3 years old son.

    Volkswagen Passat ($20,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Despite being one of the cheapest cars in Tyson Fury’s luxurious car collection as it was purchased for $20,000 only, the two-time heavyweight champion actually loves his Volkswagen Passat and considers it one of his favorites in his car collection. He has even made jokes about driving around town in his Volkswagen Passat when he retires from the boxing ring.

    Mercedes-Benz AMG G-Class SUV ($160,000)

    Via Flickr/Chris Neill

    Tyson Fury’s Mercedes Benz-AMG G class is one of the classiest in his luxurious car garage and he splurged about $150,000 on the purchase and an extra $10,000 to customize and emboss GK which stands for Gypsy King on its windows, making total of $160,000. It’s a much sort after car by most celebrities and even premier league stars due to its sturdy appearance and innovative design and style.

    Porsche Taycan ($171,000)

    Via Flickr/Eamonn and James Clarke

    The Porsche Taycan Fury decided to get after trading in his Ferrari F12 an electric car with ultra-modern attributes and luxury automobile performance. He acquired the Porsche Taycan from a dealership in Upmarket Wilmslow, Cheshire at the cost of $171,000 while he pocketed the leftover $79,000 he got from trading in his Ferrari. The Porsche Taycan boasts of a 0-60 mph in less than 5 seconds with a top speed of about 160mph

    Range Rover Sport SVR ($185,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Tyson’s Range Rover Sport happens to be an SVR meaning a Special Vehicle Operations with the R representing the most dynamic Land Rovers. The SVR features a one-engine powered by a 5.0-liter supercharged petrol V8 engine which generates 542 horsepower. Upon acquiring the Range Rover at the jaw-dropping price of $185,000, he decided to include a personal touch to it by getting it covered in camouflage decals.

    Ferrari Portofino ($260,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    Tyson Fury’s Ferrari Portofino without mincing words is considered the king of them all, the crème de la crème, a work of art on wheels and it comes at a worthwhile and worthy price of $260,000. It is considered one of the greatest proofs of prestige due to its astounding design and its lustrous and elegant lines and it holds a hidden meaning behind it as it is indistinguishable from his old friend and training mate Billy Joe Saunders’s Ferrari model. The Ferrari Portofino has a remarkable 2+2 hard-top convertible which makes it even more special and sophisticated. It boosts to 60 mph in less than 4 seconds with a top speed of 199 mph which of course cannot be tried out on public roads.

    Ferrari GTC4 Lusso ($316,000)

    Via Flickr/Terry Blackburn

    Tyson Fury considers luxurious and expensive cars as his prize for working hard in the boxing ring. And he sure knows how to splurge when it comes to rewarding himself, so, it is no surprise that he owns the Ferrari GTC4 Lusso also known as “the true prancing horse” which is one of the most expensive and most popular Ferrari models. The car is an ultra-modern deluxe car that is astonishingly pragmatic for a sports car. It is generated by a 3.9-liter V8 engine that powers 602 bhp and boosts 60 mph in less than 4 seconds. Its four-wheel drive makes it flawless for all weather conditions and it is worth a whopping sum of $307,000.

    Rolls Royce Cullinan ($376,000)

    Via Flickr/XPOSURE.COM

    The latest addition to the Rolls Royce Family is the Rolls Royce Cullinan which was launched in 2018 and has already made quite an impressionable statement in the luxurious car world. Trust Tyson Fury to cop one for his luxurious car collection at the mouth-watering price of $376,000 as it appears that he is quite the lover of Rolls Royce. The Rolls Royce Cullinan surely precedes its luxurious standards as the first-ever SUV produced by the world-renowned luxurious car brand, Rolls Royce.

    Rolls Royce Phantom ($430,000)

    Via Wikimedia Commons

    One of the most magnificent and classiest cars in the market today is the Rolls Royce Phantom which has been in existence for well over 100 years and still remains one of the most expensive and most popular cars in the league of the wealthy and mega-rich celebrities. Little wonder that Tyson Fury owns one in his luxurious collection as he is a connoisseur of luxurious and expensive cars and this car is ranked as the most expensive in his luxurious collection. This exotic car costs almost half a million dollars and its features definitely speak for its enormous price of $430,000.
