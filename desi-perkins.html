    • 2014: Perkins’ YouTube channel gains momentum, and she starts collaborating with various brands, including Sigma Beauty and Benefit Cosmetics.

    • 2016: Perkins becomes a brand ambassador for Quay Australia and launches her line of sunglasses in collaboration with the brand.
