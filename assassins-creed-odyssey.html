    This quest was a special quest for those who pre-ordered the game. Now it's also available as a Club Reward for 40 Ubi Points.

    Alexios happened upon a blind Persian who wished to see the world his friend had told him about in stories. Agreeing to be his "eyes." Alexios pledged to travel these lands on the beggar's behalf.

    Objectives of Prince of Persia

    1. Talk to the blind man

    He is in Megara in Megaris.

    3. Return to Artaxerxes

    Talk to Artaxerxes. At some point of your conversation a killer will appear. No matter which answer you choose, you will have to kill him.

    4. Kill the assassin

    5. Return to Artaxerxes

    As a reward for saving his life, he will reveal to you the place where the treasure is hidden.

    6. Find Themistokles's treasure

    Hints:

    • The treasure is in the Isle of Salamis
    • The treasure is inside the ruins
    • The ruins are south of Salamis

    7. Quest Completed

    Rewards: Epic XP, Epic Drachmae, Persian Warrior's Waistband (Epic Waist), Artaxerxes's Treasure (4125 Drachmae)
