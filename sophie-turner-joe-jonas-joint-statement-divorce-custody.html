    Joe Jonas and Sophie Turner have issued their first statement since he filed for divorce on Tuesday, September 5 after days of speculation. The long-time couple released a joint statement through their Instagram accounts, confirming their break-up and stating it was an amicable decision.

    “Statement from the two of us: 'After four wonderful years of marriage we have mutually decided to amicably end our marriage,'” the statement read. “There are many speculative narratives as to why, but truly this is a united decision and we sincerely hope that everyone can respect our wishes for privacy for us and our children,'” it continued.

    Joe Alleges Sophie Hasn’t Been Caring For Their Children

    Joe and Sophie haven’t provided a reason for their separation, but multiple reports suggest the pair may be conflicting over child-rearing, as Joe has reportedly been acting as the primary caregiver for their two daughters, despite being on tour.

    Joe formally filed for divorce from his wife on Tuesday, citing “irreconcilable differences” as the reason for their split. The paperwork was submitted in Miami-Dade County, Florida, which is where the former couple recently sold their $15 million mansion, which seemed for foreshadow their impending divorce.

    The Jonas Brothers rocker seemed to suggest that he wants a formal, split custody agreement of their two young children, as his filing mentioned that both he and Sophie have a parental responsibility to ensure the well-being of their daughters.

    However, it’s been reported that Joe has been acting as the main caregiver for their two kids.

    Speaking to TMZ, sources say Joe has taken on the primary responsibility for caring for their two young children over the last several months, even amid his band's demanding touring schedule.

    Furthermore, insiders claim that the couple has been grappling with substantial issues for a minimum of six months.

    Before releasing their joint statement, Joe was seen wearing his wedding ring on numerous occasions, even during a recent performance in Austin, Texas and in social media posts, temporarily leading some fans to think the break-up news was only speculation.

    Joe And Sophie Signed A Prenup Before Getting Married

    In 2019, Joe and Sophie had an impromptu wedding in Las Vegas, followed by an intimate ceremony at Chateau de Tourreau in the southern part of France a month later. Their first child, a daughter named Willa, was born in July 2020, and their second child arrived two years later. They have chosen not to reveal the name of their youngest child.

    It's been reported that the pair signed a prenup before their 2019 wedding, which could help make the divorce process simpler. Joe requested their prenup be upheld in his divorce filing. Joe's estimated wealth amounts to $50 million, whereas Sophie, renowned for her role as Sansa Stark in Game of Thrones, possesses a net worth of $12 million.

    There are few details available about how Joe and Sophie plan to divide their assets amid their separation as well as what their plans for custody and child support.
