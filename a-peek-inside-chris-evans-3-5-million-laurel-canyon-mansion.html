    After graduating from theater school, Chris Evans worked in several big-budget and independent movies. The actor came to prominence in 2011 when he bagged the role of Steve Rogers for Captain America: The First Avenger. Ever since he became a part of the Marvel Cinematic Universe, he became one of the highest-grossing actors, with his movies earning billions at the box office. The actor earned $75 million alone from his work as Captain America and starred in several drama and mystery movies that brought his net worth to a solid $80 million. While Evans likes to keep his personal life under the radar, he is known to make hefty purchases in real estate. After receiving the role of Captain America, he purchased a home in Hollywood Hills worth $3.5 million.

    An old home restructured for the star, Chris Evans’ mansion offers scenic views and a cozy environment for a pleasant stay.

    A Home After Fame

    Evans struggled to make a breakthrough in Hollywood as a lead role. He previously starred in the Fantastic Four movies; however, he is now best recognized for being Steve Rogers in the MCU. As mentioned by Celebrity Net Worth, Evans was paid $300,000 to star in the 2011 movie Captain America: The First Avenger. He also earned a sizable salary in the 2012 movie Avengers. After making a good income from his movies, he decided to put a price on a home in California. Evans purchased the house for $3.5 million.

    Redesigned By The Best

    Located in the iconic neighborhood of Laurel Canyon, the expensive and exclusive area is popular as many celebrities reside in the region. The home was originally constructed in the 1940s and underwent major renovations in 2013. Stewart-Gulrajani Designs, a company best known in LA to renovate and flip homes, redesigned the house before Chris Evans purchased it in the same year. Paul Wylie of Lamerica, the parent company of Stewart-Gulrajani Designs, was the listing agent for the property, while Evans was represented by Rayni Romito’s Hilton & Hyland.

    Comfortable Retreat

    According to NY Post, the home spread across nearly an acre of property with a 4,600 sq. ft. of the house in the center. Located on Torreyson Drive, the home features three bedrooms and three-and-a-half bathrooms. Two bedrooms are Master Suites, while the third bedroom serves as a Staff Quarter. The Master Bedroom has a spacious walk-in closet and an ensuite bathroom with a steam shower, radiant heat, laundry room, free-standing tub, and interior made from Italian Calcutta marble and limestone. The mansion is surrounded by gated walls from all directions and offers breathtaking views of Los Angeles and the Santa Monica mountains.

    A Seamless Indoor-Outdoor Structure

    One of the unique aspects of the home is that the indoors and outdoors merge into each other to offer a relaxing experience. The French windows and glass walls enhance the outdoors experience and offer lush views from every corner of the house. There are patios outside every room so guests can sit and enjoy the view of California.

    A Timeless Kitchen

    A home is never complete without a nice kitchen, and the mansion offers a gourmet kitchen with top-of-the-line appliances. The kitchen features stainless steel Wolf Kitchen appliances, along with a full wine fridge, a refrigerator, a built-in Miele coffee maker, custom cabinets, Italian Bianca marble countertops, and a dual Thermador dishwasher, as noted by Redfin. The cookhouse effortlessly merges with the Family Room.

    Cozy Indoors

    The homey Family Room and Living Rooms feature glass walls that drench the room in sunlight at all times. The rooms have a beige and cream interior and feature a wood fireplace combined with abstract paintings. Next to these rooms is a Dining area offering a six-person dining table. They provide outdoor patio space that has six sun loungers for the guests to enjoy the Californian sun by the pool.

    Bountiful Backyard

    A backyard is a significant aspect of any home, and Chris Evans has an equally stunning backyard as the interior of his home. Overlooking the city features a swimming pool with a spa, as reported by the LA Times. The estate also has a firepit and deck along the lines of the swimming pool under tall trees and is the most secluded spot in the house for much-needed privacy.

    Myriad Of Outdoor Amenities

    Situated in one of the finest neighborhoods in Los Angeles, Chris Evans has access to many outdoor amenities as an advantage of staying at a celebrity-only enclave. The actor has several famous neighbors around his residence, including Jack Nicholson, Jimi Hendrix, Ringo Starr, Joni Mitchell, Natalie Wood, and many more.

    Ever since the pandemic in March 2020, Chris Evans has abandoned his home to return to his hometown of Boston, Massachusetts, where he lives with his brother Scott and pet dog Dodger. He has been catching up on his reading and spending time with his family in his hometown. While the mansion remains unused, Chris Evans will be returning to Los Angeles when he begins promotion for two of his upcoming 2022 movies, The Gray Man and Lightyear.
