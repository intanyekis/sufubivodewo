    Career

    Simpkins’ journey began with quick appearances on television, swiftly graduating to feature films under the watchful eyes of industry giants. At just ten years old, he stole the show in Spielberg’s “War of the Worlds,” sharing the screen with Tom Cruise and Dakota Fanning. This early success paved the way for a string of diverse roles, showcasing his versatility across genres.

    Horror became Simpkins’ subsequent frontier, with his portrayal of the endearingly haunted Dalton Lambert in the “Insidious” franchise solidifying him as a scream king in the making. He masterfully navigated the chills and thrills, his wide-eyed innocence contrasting chillingly with the supernatural forces at play. Simpkins proved his dramatic range in independent films like “Pride and Glory,” sharing the screen with Colin Farrell and Edward Norton.

    Ty Simpkins Iron Man 3

    Simpkins is well known for playing Harley Keener in Iron Man 3, a precocious young kid who helps Tony Stark when he’s stranded in Tennessee after his Malibu mansion is destroyed. Simpkins was just 11 years old when he filmed Iron Man 3, but he held his own alongside Robert Downey Jr. His character, Harley, is intelligent, resourceful, and a big fan of Iron Man. He and Tony Stark develop a heartwarming friendship in the film.

    Ty Simpkins Endgame

    Simpkins repeated his role as Harley Keener, the young boy who helped Tony Stark in Iron Man 3, five years later. He approaches Morgan Stark, Tony’s daughter, and offers her a hug, sharing a moment of comfort and connection.
