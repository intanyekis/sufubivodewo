    Thomas Ravenel: A Politician and Reality TV Star

    Thomas Ravenel, a politician and reality television star, has a net worth of $6 million. He is a former State Treasurer of South Carolina and founder of the Ravenel Development Corporation, a commercial real estate company. Ravenel ran for the US Senate in 2004 and came in third in the Republican primary. In June 2007, Ravenel was indicted on federal cocaine distribution charges and suspended from his duties as State Treasurer. He later entered a rehabilitation facility.

    Ravenel was born on August 11, 1962 in Charleston and comes from a family with strong southern roots. His father, Arthur Ravenel, Jr., is a former South Carolina Congressman. Ravenel graduated from The Citadel in Charleston and later received his MBA from the University of South Carolina.

    Ravenel is also a cast member on the hit Bravo show, Southern Charm, and earns a salary of $25,000 per episode. He formerly dated Kathryn Dennis, who also appears on the show, and the couple has two children together. In season 2 of Southern Charm, Ravenel re-entered the political ring and ran for Senate, unsuccessfully.
