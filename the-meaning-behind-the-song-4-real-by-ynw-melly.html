    “4 Real” is a single by YNW Melly on his mixtape I AM YOU. In this song, Melly talks about being in love with a girl and wanting her, however, he often switches up the loving vibe by talking about guns and violence.

    Listening to this song, it becomes apparent that YNW Melly is conveying a feeling of conflicted emotions. He expresses his desire for a romantic connection with someone, but his references to guns and violence create a complex mix of emotions within the lyrics. This contrast adds an intriguing layer to the meaning of the song.

    For me, listening to “4 Real” brings forth memories of my own experiences with conflicting emotions. We all have moments in life where we feel torn between two different paths or emotions. This song encapsulates that feeling, with YNW Melly showcasing his vulnerability and struggle to reconcile his desires with the harsh realities of his surroundings.

    While the lyrics switch between themes of love and violence, it’s important to recognize the underlying struggles that artists like YNW Melly face. They often come from challenging backgrounds and use their music as a platform to express their emotions and experiences. “4 Real” is a prime example of this, as Melly delves into his personal battles and confronts the duality of his desires.

    Furthermore, the unreleased remix of “4 Real” featuring Lil Uzi Vert adds another layer to the song’s meaning. Collaborations like this allow artists to come together and amplify their messages through their unique styles. It’s intriguing to think about how Lil Uzi Vert’s contribution might influence the song’s overall message and impact.

    As for the production of the song, Turbo and Yung Lan deserve recognition for their contributions. The instrumental and beats play a significant role in setting the mood and enhancing the lyrics’ meaning. These producers have skillfully created a sonic backdrop that complements YNW Melly’s lyrical content, drawing listeners into his world.

    Ultimately, “4 Real” is a thought-provoking song that explores the complexities of human emotions. It showcases the real struggles and conflicts that YNW Melly has faced, using his music as a medium to express his truth. By blending themes of love and violence, he creates a captivating narrative that resonates with listeners.

    Exceptional artists like YNW Melly push the boundaries of conventional storytelling and express their raw emotions through their music. “4 Real” is just one example of the profound impact that music can have on our lives, allowing us to reflect on our own experiences and connect with others on a deeper level.

    So the next time you come across “4 Real” by YNW Melly, take a moment to appreciate the layers of meaning within the lyrics, and perhaps reflect on the conflicting emotions that you have experienced in your own life. Music has a way of bringing people together and reminding us that we are not alone in our struggles.
